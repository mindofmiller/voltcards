Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Volt Cards (“Volt Cards,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), operator of the website voltcards.us, collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our services, including our SMS/text-message deal-alert program. By using our services or opting in to receive text messages from us, you agree to this Policy.

Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to operate our services, which may include:

  • Account information — such as your email address, used to sign in and sync your data.
  • Mobile phone number — collected only from individuals who consent to receive our deal-alert text messages (see “SMS/Text Messaging” below).
  • Usage data — basic technical information (e.g., browser type) used to operate and secure the site.

How we use information

  • To provide, maintain, and secure the Volt Cards service.
  • To send trading-card deal and price-alert text messages to individuals who have opted in.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide support.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

SMS / Text Messaging

Volt Cards operates an SMS program that sends trading-card deal and price alerts to authorized recipients.

Consent

Our SMS deal-alert program is an internal program limited to authorized Volt Cards personnel. Recipients provide express consent (verbally) before their mobile number is added to the program. We do not add anyone to the program without their consent.

Message frequency

Message frequency is recurring — approximately one (1) message per day. Message frequency may vary.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Volt Cards does not charge for the text messages, but your mobile carrier’s standard message and data rates may apply based on your plan.

How we protect your mobile information — no sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your mobile phone number or your SMS opt-in/consent information with any third parties or affiliates for their marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile information may be shared only with the messaging service providers that help us deliver the text messages, and only as needed to operate the program. No mobile information will be shared with third parties for marketing.

Opting out and help

  • To stop receiving messages, reply STOP at any time. After you send STOP, we will send one confirmation message and then stop sending texts.
  • For help, reply HELP or email brett@mindofmiller.com.

Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only with service providers who help us operate our services (for example, our website host, database provider, and SMS messaging provider), and only to the extent necessary to provide those services. We may also disclose information where required by law. As stated above, mobile phone numbers and SMS consent are never shared with third parties for marketing.

Data retention and security

We retain personal information only as long as needed to provide our services or as required by law, and we use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. You may request access to or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at the email below.

Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above.
